About Me!

Welcome to my website!!!! I'm Jocilin, and I've been doing nails very casually for the last few years. This year I decided to expand my business and clientele to the public. I'm home-based and do gel-x, gel manicures, and press-on sets! I absolutely love the artistry of nails and the attention to detail it requires. I'd be so happy for y'all to book with me and hang out! If there are any questions that aren't answered on my website feel free to reach out on Instagram: @jocilin.nails

FAQ
Everything you need to know before booking
I offer a two week warranty on nails that fall off, get chipped, gems fall off, etc. Let me know and we can set up a time for me to replace and/or fix the nail.
Ultimately the decision is up to me and I can't find a general description to apply to all "simple" designs, but an example to think of would be one small heart or letter on an accent nail. Something of that degree will not incur a fee.
While I don't charge for the use of chrome powder itself, I do incur a small $2 fee for the process of chrome isolation. Isolating chrome to get a perfect finish can add a substantial amount of time to an appointment, which is where this charge comes from.
Press-on sets are more expensive because when bought, they come in a hard case to store the nails after usage and with a care bag that contains a tube of nail glue, an instruction card, and a sticker.
