Privacy Policy

What we collect

When you submit a review on this site, we collect your name, the service you received, your star rating, and your review text. Providing your Instagram handle is optional. No other personal information is collected.

How it's stored

Review submissions are stored securely using Google Firebase Firestore. Reviews are held for approval before being published publicly on the site.

Booking

Appointment booking is handled through Calendly. Any information you provide during booking is subject to Calendly's Privacy Policy.

How your data is used

Your submitted review may be displayed publicly on this website. Your information is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.
